/**
32

33 
 * includes client-server, server-server, external components and connection managers traffic.
34

35 
 * However, traffic generated by file transfers is not considered unless files were sent using
36 
 * the in-band method.
37 
 *
38 
 * @author Gaston Dombiak
39
